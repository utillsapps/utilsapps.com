Privacy Policy

My Capital & Goals

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS application My Capital & Goals (the “App”), developed by Elizaveta Peshchanskaya (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

We respect your privacy. The App is designed to work locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or store your personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users.

The App does not collect, request, or transmit information such as:

  • name;
  • email address;
  • phone number;
  • location;
  • contacts;
  • photos or media;
  • financial account credentials;
  • device identifiers;
  • usage analytics;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • crash reports sent directly to us;
  • any other personal data.

2. Local Data Storage

Any information you enter into the App, including capital-related entries, goals, notes, amounts, progress, or preferences, is stored locally on your device.

We do not have access to this data.

You are responsible for managing and protecting the data stored on your device, including through your device passcode, iCloud backup settings, or other Apple system features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data.

The App does not sell, rent, trade, or disclose personal information to advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or any other external parties.

4. Tracking and Advertising

The App does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

The App does not use third-party advertising, targeted advertising, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies.

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

The App does not include third-party analytics tools.

We do not receive or analyze user behavior, app usage statistics, or personal diagnostic information from the App.

Apple may provide developers with aggregated and anonymized information through App Store Connect or Apple system services. Such information is managed by Apple according to Apple’s own privacy policies and does not allow us to identify individual users.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services that collect personal data from users.

If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before such services are used.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 or the equivalent minimum age in other jurisdictions.

Because the App does not collect personal data, we do not knowingly collect, store, or process children’s personal information.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all user-entered data is stored locally on the user’s device, we do not retain any user data on our servers.

You can delete your data by deleting entries inside the App, if such functionality is available, or by deleting the App from your device.

Deleting the App may remove locally stored App data from your device, subject to your iOS and iCloud backup settings.

9. Security

We do not transmit or store user data on external servers.

Data entered into the App remains on your device. The security of this data depends on the security settings of your device, including your passcode, biometric authentication, operating system protections, and backup settings.

10. International Data Transfers

We do not collect or transfer user data internationally.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the App’s functionality, legal requirements, or privacy practices change.

Any updated version will be posted with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after changes means that you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
